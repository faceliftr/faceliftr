Am I eligible for a facelift?
Morpheus or Profound
Sep 13, 2020
Radiofrequency treatments can tighten skin and are more what I would consider in someone your age though I would have to see the pics to know for sure.
45 years old. Facelift or fillers?
deep plane facelift
Sep 13, 2020
I would recommend a deep plane facelift, surgery is pretty immediate in terms of result and is durable with 10-15 yrs great results. Fillers will add up over time, fill your face and change its shape to fill the loose skin and obstruct lymphatics eventually causing swelling and other issues over long term. They will cost a lot as well. A well done surgery is always a good option.
What amount of time is reasonable for time under general anesthetic (70 year old woman)?
5 hours
Sep 13, 2020
Anything longer dramatically increases risk of complications as we have seen in the aesthetic literature. Also If someone is taking that long to perform the surgery they may not be efficient and experienced enough for you. Consider Sedation as well though I usually do deep plane facelift in 2.5 hrs general Anaesthesia.

I have olive tone skin. Will a facelift leave me visible scars?
Scars come from tension
Sep 13, 2020
A deep plane facelift removes all tension from the skin, without tension the incisions do not stretch and do not become visible scars. Its all about good technique
