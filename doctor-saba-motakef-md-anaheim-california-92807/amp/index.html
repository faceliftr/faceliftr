Ideas for treating my gummy smile?
Botox for gummy smile
Sep 12, 2020
Botox is a simple and every effective treatment for gummy smile. Precise, low dose injections of Botox that target the lip elevators (the muscles that elevate the upper lip) can reduce the amount of gingival display, creating a softer, more natural smile. This treatment will last 3-4 months. Unlike the other treatments you listed, this treatment is not permanent.

Does Juvederm pose any risk or complications when removing a wisdom tooth?
Dental work after Juvederm Injections
Oct 12, 2019
Transient bacteremia (bacteria entering your bloodstream) can occur after dental work is performed. It is possible that this bacteria can then seed an injected hyaluronic acid filler and increase your risk of infection. For this reason, I generally recommend that patients wait a minimum of two weeks after fillers are performed before dental work is done. In your case, you are 8 months out, so you should be fine.
