Does a Chin Implant set off metal detectors?
Chin implants and metal detectors
Dec 15, 2016
Typically the screws that are used (if at all) for chin implants are made of titanium.  Titanium is not ferromagnetic and therefore will not set off metal detectors.  They will show up on x-rays and other imaging modalities though.  You'd need to check with your doctor to ask what they used to secure the implant.
