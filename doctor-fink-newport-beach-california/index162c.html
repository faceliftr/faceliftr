Kybella or lipo for my double chin?
Kybella vs Liposuction
Jun 18, 2020
Kybella is a non-surgical option for younger patients with good skin elasticity. It requires 3 or more treatments spaced 6 weeks apart. Repeated swelling accompanies each treatment. Liposuction is accomplished in one procedure and although I have my patients wear a support garment for a week, the swelling is often less. Incisions are minimal. Liposuction also allows better contouring in my experience.
