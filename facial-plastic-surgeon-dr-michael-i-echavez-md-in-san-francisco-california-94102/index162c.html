Sculptra for mouth area
Sep 28, 2020
I think that Sculptra could help improve the area on the side of your mouth. The placement of the Sculptra would be important to obtain the best result though, so I would be sure to see a physician who has a lot of experience with Sculptra.
Which order is best If you are using two different surgeons, facelift then fat transfer or vice
Sequencing of facelift and fat transfer
Sep 28, 2020
An examination or photos of you would be helpful to give you the best advice as to the order of scheduling a facelift and fat transfer. If you do not have a lot of excess skin, sometimes adding volume to the face can provide enough improvement so that a facelift would not be needed. If you have a lot of skin laxity with volume loss, then I would recommend having the facelift first and the fat transfer afterwards.
Does CoolSculpt affect tattoos?
CoolSculpting over tattoos
Sep 26, 2020
I have performed CoolSculpting over tattoos without any problem. The tattoo should not affect the results of the CoolSculpting or the ability to have the treatment.
Can I get microneedling a few days after Sculptra?
Micro needling after Sculptra
Sep 26, 2020
There should not be any problem with having a micro needling treatment after a Sculptra treatment. The micro needling should not affect the Sculptra results. There are many different types of micro needling. Some use radio frequency and some do not. There are also different systems and depths, so it is difficult to say if monthly micro needling treatments might be bad for the skin. If the treatments are performed correctly, they should not cause any scaring.
I have become resistant to Botox on my neck. What do you suggest?
Botox no longer working on neck
Sep 25, 2020
If someone has developed antibodies to Botox, there would be no effect wherever it was used. Since you are only not seeing results on your neck, while seeing results on your face, it is likely that this is an issue with dosing. You most likely just need more Botox in your neck to obtain your desired results.
Sculptra or Voluma?
Sculptra or Voluma?
Sep 25, 2020
I think it is possible to achieve similar results with Voluma or Sculptra, but results from Sculptra generally last longer than those from Voluma. I have used Sculptra in patients in their 70's and have seen good results. I would recommend following the advice of the physician who would be treating you though, as your physician may have a different experience with fillers and perhaps has had better results with Voluma over Sculptra in more mature patients.
Is CoolSculpting harmful for the belly button?
Is CoolSculpting harmful to the belly button?
Sep 24, 2020
CoolSculpting should not affect the belly button when a procedure is done over this area. It is important to examine the patient first though, because if there is a hernia in the belly button, the procedure should not be done to that area. CoolSculpting over an area with a hernia could cause damage.

CoolSculpting versus liposuction

CoolSculpting and liposuction both provide permanent fat reduction. More fat can be removed in a single procedure with liposuction than with CoolSculpting though. If the recovery time is not an issue for you, I would recommend liposuction to remove fat from you arms, abdomen and flanks, as this should give you more dramatic results than CoolSculpting. CoolSculpting with large scar on abdomen
Sep 24, 2020
An examination of you would really be needed to determine if you might be a candidate for a CoolSculpting treatment while having a large scar on the abdomen. Is it safe to get filler and Botox 3-5 days after microneedling or non-ablative laser?
Filler and Botox after micro needling or non-ablative laser
Sep 24, 2020
It is possible that there could still be some swelling present 3-5 days after micro needling or a non-ablative laser treatment to the face. If there was swelling, I would recommend not having any fillers yet. I would not see an issue with a Botox treatment though. If there was not any swelling, I think having Botox and a filler would be okay 3-5 days after micro needling or non-ablative laser treatment.
How long should I wait to remove a chin implant?
Timing of chin implant removal
Sep 23, 2020
Technically, a chin implant could be removed anytime after it was placed. It normally does take several months for most of the swelling to subside after a chin implant is inserted, so the appearance of the area will be affected for a few months. I would recommend waiting at least 3 months to have a better idea of your results before having the implant removed.

Would Botox help with facial asymmetry?
Sep 22, 2020
If your facial asymmetry is due to your right masseter muscle being larger than the left, it is possible that Botox could help improve the asymmetry.
