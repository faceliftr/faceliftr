With a tummy tuck, are progressive tension sutures permanent or dissolvable?
Sep 13, 2020
Progressive tension sutures, also called quilting sutures, are dissolvable sutures used to the cavity created when the skin and muscle layer are elevated off the muscle. This is done to decrease the space where fluid can collect after an abdominoplasty. Some dimpling can be seen early on after the procedure, but this goes away as the skin relaxes and the sutures dissolve.
