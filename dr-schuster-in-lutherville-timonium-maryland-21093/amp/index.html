Extreme Hanging Columella! Please help. Which surgical procedures can be used to fix this?
Hanging columella
Sep 22, 2020
Yes, you have excessive columellar show. Your tip shape suggest that you have malrotation of your tip cartilages. This means they are oriented more vertically than horizontally. This is important because it generally results in weakness of the nostril rims, which if not corrected, can lead to notching after surgery. Reducing the hanging columella is a fairly routine part of rhinoplasty. I recommend going forward with some live consultations to get everything fixed. Good luck.

Is this gynecomastia or not?
Gynecomastia
Sep 21, 2020
It appears from the photo that you have enlarged breasts which by definition is gynecomastia. usually this is due to abnormally enlarged breast tissue (glandular gynecomastia) though could also be due to only fat (pseudogynecomastia). treatment is essentially the same, removal of the excess tissue. The difference is that your surgeon must always be prepared to excise true glandular tissue which will not come out with just liposuction.
