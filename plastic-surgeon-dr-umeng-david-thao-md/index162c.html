Butt shrinking after BBL procedure
Nov 20, 2018
Thanks for your question.  Your buttocks can swell up quite a bit after your BBL surgery. After a few weeks, the swelling will subside therefore making your butt smaller than when you first came out of surgery. Secondly, not all of the fat injected will survive and will get reabsorbed by your body.  The best thing you can do is to avoid pressure and trauma on your buttocks and also follow up closely with your surgeon to discuss any concerns or issues.  Best wishes!
