Is it normal to have a 400cc implant completely removed and drains placed under local anesthesia?
Implant removal is possible under local anesthesia.
Aug 16, 2017
If you are simply removing the implants and no capsule tissue, then it is very easy to do under local. If however you are having the capsule removed at the same time, local would be painful for you to tolerate. Drains are usually used when the capsule is removed and not necessary when only the implants are removed. Good luck!
