Mommy makeover and BBL. Should I get it all at once, or wait?
BBL, breast surgery and TT
Sep 27, 2020
Without in person evaluation or series of good photos, it is hard to give you good advice. In general, BBL, which usually involves liposuction of torso and fat transfer for shaping buttocks and hips, should be done separately if large volume liposuction and large augmentation is anticipated. In the case of smaller liposuction/augmentation, depending of how extensive other MMO procedures are, it can be considered as a single surgery in properly selected candidates. Good luck.

I’m 18 and 5’7 and I’m 235 pounds. Will I qualify to get a BBL?
BBL candidate and BMI
Sep 27, 2020
Patient with BMI of 36.81 are poor candidates for any body contouring surgery. Not only that risks of surgery are 3-4 times higher than in average population but also change that can be created removing 4 liters of fat (based on State of Florida law) will not make desired effect. It would be in your best interest to lose 30-40 lb before considering such a surgery. Good luck.
I am currently a 32 C. I am 5’4" and 125 lbs. Is there any way I could reduce my breast size to a sm
Small breast reduction
Sep 26, 2020
It would be very simple to reduce your breast for one cup size; very likely this can be done using lollipop incision (vertical mammoplasty). We do most of our reductions with liposuction since mature breast tissue has 70-90% of fat content. That technique better preserves innervation and blood supply, what in the case of small breast like yours may not be so important. Skin excision (removal) is done based on skin redundancy after gland reduction (lollipop or inverted T).
