Which would you recommend; Under or over muscle implant?
Cost should be the same, and under-muscle is safer
Feb 10, 2018
Hello,In my practice cost does not change with above of below muscle implant placement. It has everything to do with safety.  In looking at your anatomy, a breast lift is definitely needed with your augmentation. For safety reasons implants should be placed under the muscle in order to preserve the blood flow to your nipples and breast skin to ensure proper wound healing. Under-muscle placement also protects the nerves to the nipples so that numbness is less likely. Best of luck!

Rhinoplasty Swelling
Patience is a virtue
Feb 10, 2018
Hello,The 3 main factors with persistent swelling are (1) if you had an extensive procedure (with osteotomies and major reshaping), (2) if you had an “open” rhinoplasty (visible scar underneath the nose), and (3) if it you had a corrective rhinoplasty, then swelling can last many months. Although certain easy things can help, such as elevation, icing and a taping regimen, the most important is time.  Patiently waiting for your tissues to heal is the only effective strategy.  Best of luck.
