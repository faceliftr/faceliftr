Scars
Jan 21, 2020
You are still very early in your stages of healing.  Scars take months to fully settle.  It is very normal to experience scar tissue at this point. Yes, massage can help soften them.  They will soften and fade more as time goes on.  There are also treatment options and products to help with the scars and you can discuss this with your surgeon.  All the best.

Breast reduction with needed lift and tummy tuck. Am I too old for these procedures?
Mommy Makeover
Jan 14, 2020
You are definitely not too old for a mommy makeover.  Age is not a problem as long as you are in good health.  You surgeon will run tests prior to surgery such as blood work, EKG, ect. to ensure it will be safe to operate.  This is usually done with patients of any age.  Make sure to consult with a board certified plastic surgeon who can determine the best plan for you.  Good luck!
