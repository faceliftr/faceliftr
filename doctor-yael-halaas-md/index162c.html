Can having finasteride do harm for family planning?
Hair Loss Treatment
Mar 25, 2019
There are no clinical findings that taking finasteride will negatively impact family planning.However, if you suffer from a low sperm count, taking it could lower it further. Please seekconsultation with a hair loss specialist for more information.
Telogen effluvium or normal shedding?
Hair Loss Treatment
Mar 25, 2019
It sounds like you are experiencing an acceleration in androgenic alopecia. The medicationthat you were taking may have slowed its progression but now that you have stopped. Irecommend seeking consultation with a board-certified hair restoration specialist.
How to prevent genetic hair loss at the age of 20 if we have healthy hair before losing or thinning hai
Hair Loss Treatment
Mar 25, 2019
Unless your hair loss stems from an underlying medical condition, it’s not possible to preventhair loss. However, there are treatments that can slow the rate in which your hair is thinning andeven replace what you have lost. PRP, topical minoxidil and oral medication have shownpromising results. I recommend consultation with hair restoration specialist for evaluation andtreatment.
Will Rogaine combined with other topical treatments help more with Female AGA than Rogaine alone?
Hair Loss Treatment
Mar 25, 2019
Treatment for hair loss is different for each patient. Some respond well to Rogaine while othersexperience continued hair loss. The goal is to determine why your hair is falling out and whatcan be done to slow the progression. I suggest an in-office consultation with a hair loss specialist.
I've been using 1 ml Minoxidil a day for 6 months but my hair is still falling out. Should I continue i
Hair Loss
Mar 25, 2019
If you’ve been using minoxidil for six months without a change in the rate of hair loss,unfortunately it may not work for you. However, there are other types of treatments you can try,which include oral medication, PRP, laser therapy and even hair restoration. I recommendseeking consultation with a hair restoration specialist for evaluation and possible treatment.
What can I do about my male pattern baldness? I’m on finasteride but I know it won’t last forever.
Hair Loss Treatment
Mar 25, 2019
If you are suffering from male pattern baldness, you need to be proactive. The focus is to slowthe thinning process and finasteride is a good option. In addition, you can also try topicalminoxidil and laser therapy. PRP injections may be beneficial as well.
Minoxidil does not seem to work anymore. Please read question details. What could be the reason?
Hair Loss Treatment
Mar 25, 2019
Although Minoxidil is shown to stop hair loss in some people, results to vary. Long-term effectsare not the same for every person. In addition, it is possible for it to lose its efficacy over time. Isuggest scheduling a consultation with a hair loss specialist who can evaluate the cause of yourhair loss and determine the best course of action.
Alopecia areata. Why can't I get corticosteroid injections?
Hair Loss Treatment
Mar 25, 2019
The two treatments actually work opposite of each other. The squaric acid can cause redness andinflammation whereas corticosteroids work to reduce swelling, inflammation and dermatologicsymptoms. I suggest scheduling a consultation with a hair restoration specialist who can examine and go over your complete medical history.
What’s the best facelift? Is it better to have a lower face lift and then a brow lift?
Facelift
Mar 25, 2019
If you are planning on having both procedures performed, I suggest doing them at the same time.You then only have one recovery period. It is important that you consult with a board-certifiedfacial plastic surgeon who specializes in facial rejuvenation.
Sunken mid-face after weight loss. Do I need a facelift? I'm only 27.
Facelift
Mar 25, 2019
Loss of volume in the face after weight loss is common. If you feel that you look “sunken in”,you can replace lost volume with dermal fillers. Voluma works well at restoring lost volume inthe midface, which will lift your cheeks.
Does a mid-face (cheek) lift also take care of lower eyebags?
Facelift
Mar 25, 2019
As we get older, our cheeks start to look deflated, giving us a saggy midface and jawline. Amidface lift can correct lost volume in the cheeks but will not address under eye bags. If you have excess fatty deposits under your eyes, you could consider a lower blepharoplasty. On the other hand, if you are only volume deficient and have hollowness under your eyes, a midface lift in conjunction with filler in the tear troughs will give you a refreshed, more youthful appearance.
How many Facelift surgeries do surgeons do in a day?
Facelift
Mar 25, 2019
Hello and thank you for this interesting question. The number of procedures a plastic surgeonperforms will vary according to type and duration. Some schedule more than one per day, whileothers limit the number of full facelifts they perform. As a rule of thumb, your surgeon shouldnot be overbooked for invasive procedures.
Would a facelift address my chubby cheeks and deep nasolabial folds?
Facelift
Mar 25, 2019
Facelifts do work well to reposition skin that has lost its elasticity due to age or genetics.However, at 30 years old, you should possibly consider less invasive procedures such as facialcontouring with dermal fillers, Botox, or Silhouette Instalift.
