Tummy tuck or liposculpture?
Tummy Tuck vs Liposuction
Aug 24, 2020
Thank you for your question. Liposuction reduces fat and a tummy tuck removes loose skin. If you perform liposuction alone, the loose skin will still be present. In most cases, there is some skin contraction following liposuction. You should discuss these options with your plastic surgeon.
Does a TT help with urinary incontinence?
Tummy Tuck and urinary incontinence
Aug 24, 2020
Thank you for your question. A tummy tuck removes extra abdominal skin and fat and tightens the abdominal wall. This procedure does not affect the bladder or the urethra. So no, this procedure will not help with urinary incontinence.
