Does Hyaluronidase also dissolve some of your facial fat along with the filler?
Effect ogf hyaluronidase
Sep 16, 2020
Hyaluronidase will dissolve the hyaluronic acid alone, it will not affect fat. If an area has been treated with hyaluronic acid gel into an area that has fat, you might see the shrinking of the fullness of the combination but it is more like not dissolving the fat. Our bodies make and also dissolve hyaluronic acid. The production slows with age but th edissolving of the body made HA continues at its normal speed.
