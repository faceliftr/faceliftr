Will lowering the breast fold lift the nipple at all?
Changing the breast fold may change the nipple position.
Dec 20, 2018
The nipple position and the breast fold are indeed related, so in some cases, lowering the breast fold will cause the nipple (and areola) to rotate upwards, giving the appearance of elevation. It is impossible to know if this is what you need, but you should seek the advice of a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss. There are other options for elevating the nipple, but these may involve varying degrees of scarring.
