How far can the wings on a chin implant go back?
Extended Chin Implant
Sep 28, 2020
If you are wanting to truly widen or build out the mandible behind the chin, you would need an extended custom chin implant design to do so. The wings of standard chin implants are there to provide a feathering of the implant into the bone behind the chin to look more natural. Their purpose is not to build out the jawline behind it.
Will Permalip implants give me permanent volume?
Lip Implants
Sep 26, 2020
Permalip is the contemporary version of lip implants. Made of an ulltraosft solid silicone material their volume is permanent. The issue with lip implants is not whether their volume is permanent but whether their location in the lips will feel acceptable to the patient.

Facial Surgery and Accutane
Sep 26, 2020
While Accutane affects skin healing it would be reasonable to assume that even intraoral mucosal healing could also be affected. While this risk is low there is no reason to take any unnecessary changes in elective aesthetic surgery. You should wait at least three months being off Accutane before proceeding with jaw implants or buccal lipectomies.
