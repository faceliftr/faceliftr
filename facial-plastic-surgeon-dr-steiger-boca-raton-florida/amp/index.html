Will my nose become more slim over time? 16 days post rhinoplasty.
Healing after rhinoplasty
Jun 29, 2018
Swelling after rhinoplasty dose take a year or longer to resolve.  The swelling will appear as if your nose is wider/fatter and less defined than you want it to be.  The thicker the skin, the longer it takes for the nose swelling to go away.  It is a process that takes time, however you should try to focus on enjoying the improvements that you already have and let nature take its course. 
Will it be hard to show facial expression after endoscopic forehead lift?
Endoscopic Forehead Lift
Jun 29, 2018
Long-term the endoscopic forehead lift should not change your facial expression.  The procedure does raise your brows to a more elevated position, so yes that will change. However, the muscle motion should be the same as before once the swelling is resolved if the muscles are not cut during the procedure.

Do I have to remove orbital rim implants before getting facelift?
Facelift surgery and implants
Mar 31, 2017
Orbital rim implants are not commonly performed today. However, it should not be necessary to remove your already in place implants before or during facelift surgery.  If you are happy with the way they appear then you should keep them. I have performed a facelift on a woman with a simialr circumstance and she looks great and is very happy with her results.
I am planning on getting a facelift under general anesthesia and I had an abnormal EKG
Medical Clearance for Facelift Surgery
Mar 31, 2017
With your abnormal EKG we would have you see a Cardiologist in order to obtain a  full workup and medical/cardiology clearance for elective surgery. We perform our facelifts under IV sedation, not general anesthesia, but would still require cardiology clearance for your abnormal EKG.  It is always best to be safe!

Swelling 2 Weeks and 2 Days After Facelift, When Can I Expect this to go Down?
Bruising After Facelift Surgery
Mar 31, 2017
Bruising and healing can be quite variable from person to person.  We all heal along a bell curve. What this means is that you have some people that never get bruising and some people that can take a month or more for bruising to resolve.  The vast majority of our patients have most of their bruising resolved withing 2-3 weeks after surgery.  It sounds as if you are right within that 2-3 week stage and that the little bit of neck bruising will resolve within the next week.
