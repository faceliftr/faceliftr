Bruising after Juvederm injection
Aug 18, 2015
It is very common to bruise after a Juvederm injection and it should subside shortly. Before your next injection – try discontinuing products such as aspirin, fish oil, etc that are known to thin the blood and increase the chance of bruising. Try to stay patient as the bruising subsides.Mark Petroff, M.D., FACS
