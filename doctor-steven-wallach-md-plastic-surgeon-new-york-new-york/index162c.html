Can I get a face fat transfer if I had Voluma 6 months ago and Restylane Lyft last month?
Facial fillers
Jun 24, 2020
The voluma may last about 12 months or so.  You can get fat injection.  Fat injection does not take completely 1:1. I always tell my patients that this  may need to be repeated several times to achieve the desired full effect.  If you have lax skin as well, you may need that treated as well.  Also remember that as the voluma dissolves you may aldo get some deflation which may require more fat grafting.
Smart Lipo versus VASER Lipo?
Lipo
Jun 24, 2020
It is not the wand( the machine) its the magician ( the surgeon) that creates all the magic.  I have used just about all the machines, and I think that you can get a nice result with just about all of them. I do not think the heating systems like smartlipo or vaser offer any advantage because the amount of skin tightening I have seen in my opinion is negligible.

Drain or no drain for a tummy tuck?
drains or not
May 12, 2020
I usually use drains for tummy tucks.  They do not prevent hematomas but help to remove fluid that can accumulate in the area.  Some surgeons will use quilting sutures without drains in attempt to eliminate the dead space.

Can I do just lipo or is a bra line lift necessary? Weight loss
Back rolls
May 1, 2020
It is highly likely that liposuction alone will not significantly change the upper back and hanging roll of skin. I have treated this area many, many times with liposuction and while it will reduce the fat to some extent, inevitably that skin will still hang. It is a trade off between an upper back lift/ long incision vs contour improvement. That is a personal choice you have to determine for yourself, but an exam in person is essential to formulate a plan with you. Best of luck.
