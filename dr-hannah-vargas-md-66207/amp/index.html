Skin around hair transplant recipient site is darker than my actual skin color, 3 weeks post op. (Pho
skin discoloration
May 16, 2018
Darker skin types can have skin darkening around the transplant sites post surgery.  This is usually caused from inflammation..  It should fade over time to a lighter color of your normal skin, make sure to try to avoid direct sun exposure during the healing process to avoid any extra hyperpigmintation of the area.
Is it normal for one or two hair grafts to come out after a hair transplant?
Shedding
May 16, 2018
Your transplanted grafts will normally shed 1-3 weeks post transplant.  If you are seeing a graft 24-48 hours after then it may have been displaced by bumping the area.  If it is only a few grafts I wouldn't worry about it, of course follow up with your Dr. if you have any post op concerns.  
Gym after hair transplant?
Gym
May 16, 2018
You can continue regular exercise, it will not impact your hair transplant after 45 days.  The grafts are like your normal hair at this point.
Is this normal 1 month post FUE that there's no shedding?
Shedding
May 7, 2018
At one month I would not be too concerned with our lack of shedding.  Everyone can have different healing time from surgery, Your new hairs will grow regardless.

How should I sleep 10 days post hair transplant?
Sleeping
Apr 26, 2018
Most can return to normal sleeping positions 7-10 days post op.  If there was no bleeding you most likely did not cause any harm to your new grafts.  Of course, see your Dr. if you have any concerns.  
I gotten an hair transplant 3 weeks ago and my hair came out with the grafts.
Shedding
Apr 26, 2018
Do you mean the hair fell out of the grafted areas?  The hair shaft  shedding from the transplanted follicle is normal.  The grafts coming out is not. The hair will shed and the follicle will stay in the scalp and re-grow a new hair in 6 months to 1 year.  See your Dr. if you have any post op concerns.  
Is there any chance for my hair to regrow on my balding spots? I am 18
Hair Loss
Apr 26, 2018
If your hair loss is not medically related and just from normal aging it most likely will not re-grow in the bald areas.  See a Dr. to discuss your hair restoration options and plan for the future.  
How much will it cost for each hair graft? Or how much will 1600 grafts cost?
Price scale
Apr 26, 2018
Price per graft can depend on location and type of transplant you want. In our market we see price range from $4-7 a graft for under 2000 grafts.   Speak with a hair transplant Dr. to discuss your goals and get an accurate cost.  
What Norwood scale is this? The first pic is me at 40 the second is when I was 28 years.
Varies
Apr 26, 2018
It may not be possible to achieve your year old self hairline or density.  You will need more than one transplant to achieve your desired look,  Speak to a hair restoration Dr. about all your options and what a realistic expectation may be.  You can have a successful transplant and outcome with the right planing.  
Hair transplant uneven after 3.5 months?
Give it time
Apr 26, 2018
A little too soon to evaluate your true result.  Give it 1 year and then address any concerns to your Dr.  Hair grows in different cycles.
