Can I restore my original nose?
Removing bridge rib graft
Sep 14, 2020
It's hard to say whether your bridge would return to it's original appearance after removing your bridge rib graft.
The main consideration is how well your skin would shrink wrap back down over the underlying bone. This varies depending on your skin thickness and age.

Eating and drinking instructions before rhinoplasty?
How long to fast before rhinoplasty
Aug 26, 2020
I typically tell my adult patients not to eat or drink anything for 8 hours prior to their surgery. However, you should listen to what your surgeon/surgery facility tell you to do as they would know what would be best for your specific circumstances.
The reason for the fasting is to make sure your stomach is as empty as possible before having the anesthesia so as to prevent any stomach contents from coming up your feeding tube (esophagus) and then down into your lungs.
