Is there a specific diet I need to follow before a tummy tuck with lipo and breast lift?
Preop Diet?
Feb 25, 2020
Hello and thank you for your question. I normally recommend my patients to eat healthy prior to surgery, not necessarily going on a diet, since during the recovery time most patients spend more time at home, don’t exercise and don’t follow their diet, so there is a potential for a weight rebound post operatively. So I would rather change your eating habits to a healthy balanced diet these coming months and continue it after the surgery.
I hope this helps
