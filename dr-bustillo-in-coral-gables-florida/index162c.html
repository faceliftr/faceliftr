How long does it take for facelift scars to mostly disappear?
scars becoming imperceptable
Mar 28, 2019
If properly executed, the scars after a facelift will become imperceptible by week 6. If there was tension left on the skin, then it may take much longer.
Hair Loss 2 Months After Surgery Normal?
hair loss after facelift
Mar 28, 2019
This can occur and happens from the trauma of the surgery. Rogaine works very well for "shock" hair loss.
How Long/How Important Facelift Compression Garment?
compression garment
Mar 28, 2019
I personally do not believe that these garments provide anything of value after a facelift. The initial bandage is removed after 1 days and then a small dressing for 2 days. That is our protocol.
What is a Weekend Facelift?
weekend facelift
Mar 28, 2019
I would not recommend a "weekend" facelift. For good long-lasting results, a comprehensive facelift is the ideal procedure.
How Long Until Swelling Is 100% Gone After Face Lift?
swelling after a facelift
Mar 28, 2019
I would say it takes about 3 months for all of the swelling to subside. It may take longer if fat injections were placed.
Angry red area from popping out stitches after facelift and platysmaplasty. Would it be safe
sutures pooping out after facelift
Mar 28, 2019
I think the best thing to do would be to see your surgeon and let him make recommendation based on his clinical findings.
What is a difference in recovery between the just fat transfer and face/neck lift plus fat tr
difference in recovery
Mar 28, 2019
The difference is substantial. The swelling from fat transfer is usually only in the face and subsides much faster than in a facelift. I would say there is about a 2 week difference.
What happens when you have a suture abscess from internal sutures that connect the muscle tis
suture abcess
Mar 28, 2019
If the sutures were absorbable, then the area will heal fine with local wound care. If it is a permanent suture, then it may or may not heal with wound care. If it doesn't, the area may have to be re-explored.
How long after a facelift can I get temple fillers?
temple fillers after facelift
Mar 28, 2019
I would recommend waiting  6-8 weeks before having any fillers done to the face after a facelift, so that most of the initial swelling has subsided. The injector can then better judge how much to place.
How long after a facelift is it safe to color my hair?
hair color after facelift
Mar 28, 2019
We recommend that the patient wait 6 weeks after surgery. The incisions behind the ear must be well healed so that the chemicals do not harm them.
Do you think it's a good idea to massage your face and neck after a facelift? What are pros a
massaging after facelift
Mar 28, 2019
I do encourage massaging certain areas in an upward circular motion to help reduce swelling. No harm can be done.
Can I use ice to bring down the swelling around my eyes? How long till the swelling go down ?
ice for swelling
Mar 28, 2019
Yes, Ice will help reduce the swelling after facial surgery. Just be careful not to burn you skin with it.
Double chin, marionette lines and lump 3 weeks after facelift and neck lift. I am interested
swelling after facelift hematoma
Mar 28, 2019
This is common after having a hematoma. Talk with your surgeon. He may suggest massages to the area to help the swelling subside faster.
Wouldn't a facelift really make my eye wrinkles more visible?
will facelift make eyelids wrinkle?
Dec 11, 2018
No. A well performed facelift will not cause the wrinkles you are seeing when you elevate the skin with your hands. This happens with your hands because you are pulling the skin. The facelift elevates the deeper layers and does not pull on the skin. This is a very common question!
Mid face lift after fillers?
dissolve filler before surgery
Dec 11, 2018
Fillers will not affect the result of the surgery...unless you have a great amount of fillers in the cheeks. This can make it a bit more difficult in achieving elevation because of the weight of the tissues.
