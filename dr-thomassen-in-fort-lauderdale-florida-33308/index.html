Can I convert my mini tummy tuck to a full tummy tuck?
Tummy tuck
Nov 14, 2019
Yes, there are actually very few candidates that do well with just a mini tummy tuck.  Most patients will benefit from a full tummy tuck and you should have no problem undergoing a full tummy tuck.  
A Capsular Contracture MUST be operated right away after it was noticed?
Capsular Contracture
Nov 5, 2019
A capsular contracture is not an emergency.  You can wait to have it corrected, but the correction usually involves a partial capsulectomy and implant exchange.  If you have 17 year old implants, these should be exchanged at this point if they are silicone gel implants. 
Would brow bone reduction help me?
Deep Radix
Nov 5, 2019
Your issue is not a prominent brow but a deep radix, which is where the nose originates.  A small amount of filler will help reduce this depression and give you a nicer profile.  Digital imaging can be done in the office so you can see the effect this would have.  
Can my misplaced areola complex be moved down? How extensive is the surgery and is skin graf
Moving areola down
Nov 5, 2019
The nipple-areola complex cannot be moved down.  However, the breast can be reduced further, removing lower pole fullness.  This would have the effect of placing the nipple-areola more on the center of the breast mound rather than slightly higher than the breast mound as it is now.  
Would a mini tummy tuck affect a previous double hernia scar?
Tummy Tuck
Nov 5, 2019
The tummy tuck scar would be made just at or under the prior scar and the tissue removed with the tummy tuck would eliminate that prior scar so you should only have one scar afterwards. 
What eye surgery would I need to raise up my lower eye lids?
Midface lift
Nov 5, 2019
An endoscopic midface lift would lift the cheek pad utilizing a small incision inside your temporal hairline.  This procedure is done under IV sedation.

Eyelid Surgery
Nov 5, 2019
I recommend you obtain clearance from your ophthalmologist.  An upper eyelid blepharoplasty can exacerbate dry eye symptoms so having a therapy in place for 1-2 weeks that can treat these symptoms is important.  I would not recommend a lower lid blepharoplasty at the same time as this can further worsen dry eye symptoms.
