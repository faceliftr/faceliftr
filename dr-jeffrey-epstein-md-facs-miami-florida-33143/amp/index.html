The ISHRS is a good start, but there are minimal requirements to be a member. FISHRS is a much higher qualification, meaning the surgeon is truly committed to performing hair restoration surgeon. Options to fix depleted donor area?
Depleted donor supply
Jan 8, 2020
A growing number of patients are presenting with overharvested and or scarred donor areas from FUE.  To repair this, most often SMP provides the best outcome, however sometimes grafting with beard hairs can help fill these areas in.  Finally, prevention is always key –  make sure your surgeon does your procedure and not designate it to per diem technicians who may never see you again, and uses the best FUE devices and punches which reduce scarring.
