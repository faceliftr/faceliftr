Can I get rid of the veins under my eyes? I’m 22.
Under Eye Veins
Jul 3, 2018
Under eye laser treatment and eye fillers may help to reduce the appearance of under eye veins. Lasers pose a slight risk of clotting and serious eye damage. Although these are rare possibilities, they are potential side effects and therefore many patients and surgeons opt for the use of injectable fillers such as Restylane. This puts a barrier between the areas of less volume and the visible under eye veins and helps to minimize the greenish/blueish tint and increase volume.
